01 · The idea

A workbench, not a stage

Direction B treats Nous as a beautifully made hand tool: one excellent sans (Instrument Sans) doing every job through weight and size alone, zinc neutrals warmed two degrees toward violet, and a single signature device — the index mark, a small machined violet bar that indicates the active thing everywhere: sidebar page, tab, palette row, focused cell. Elevation is real (edge highlight + layered shadow), motion is quick and physical (things compress when pressed), and nothing decorates.

02 · Color tokens

Zinc, warmed toward violet

The complete --color-* contract, both modes — paste-ready for COLOR_SCHEMES. Neutrals carry a 2° violet cast so the accent never sits on foreign grey; the accent itself is a tuned working violet, calmer than Catppuccin's.
