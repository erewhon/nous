Nous

Where thinking takes form

Explore View Source

A personal knowledge system where research, reflection, and networked thinking converge. Your notes aren't files—they're an evolving structure of ideas, each one linked to the last, each one leading to the next.

Connections

Networked Thinking

Wiki-links, backlinks, and an interactive knowledge graph surface connections you didn't know existed. Ideas don't live in silos.

Research

AI-Powered Inquiry

Search the web, extract content, synthesize sources. A research assistant that works alongside your thinking, not instead of it.

Collaboration

Think Together

Start a live session on any page. Share a link. Edit simultaneously. No accounts required—just a URL and an idea.

Memory

Spaced Repetition

Turn any note into flashcards. A built-in review system that makes knowledge stick, with streak tracking and adaptive scheduling.

Reflection

Daily Practice

Daily notes with goal tracking, energy check-ins, and habit streaks. Build a rhythm of thinking that compounds over time.

Publishing

Share Your Work

Publish notebooks as themed websites. Minimal, academic, documentation, or blog styles. Your knowledge, your presentation.

Built on conviction

The principles that shape every decision in Nous

I

Local-first, always

Your data lives on your machine. Plain files, no proprietary databases, no cloud dependency. You own every byte. Sync when you choose to, not because you have to.

II

Native performance

Built with Rust and Tauri. Not an Electron wrapper. Not a web app pretending to be native. Fast startup, low memory, real desktop integration across macOS, Linux, and Windows.

III

Thinking over tooling

Every feature serves the act of thinking. No feature bloat, no productivity theater. The interface recedes so your ideas can advance.

Open source

Nous is free software. Read every line, modify anything, contribute back. Built in the open because knowledge tools should be as transparent as the thinking they enable.
